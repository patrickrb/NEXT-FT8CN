Excluded callsign prefixes. After FT8CN decodes a message, callsigns matching these prefixes will not be added to the "Call" list, preventing the auto-caller from calling those callsigns.
Multiple callsign prefixes can be entered, separated by commas or spaces.
The excluded callsign prefix action has the highest priority, taking precedence over "Followed," "CQ," and "Directed to me."
