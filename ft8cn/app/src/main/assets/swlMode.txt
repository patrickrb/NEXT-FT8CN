SWL stands for Shortwave Listener.
SWL mode is a receive-only mode where you listen to contacts between other amateur radio operators without transmitting.
For SWL, FT8CN adds "Save Decoded Messages" and "Save SWL Records" features.
Save Decoded Messages: Saves all messages decoded by FT8CN.
Save SWL Records: Saves the QSO logs of contacts between other amateur radio stations that you monitored. In FT8CN, a successful SWL QSO requires: a closing message (73, RR73, or RRR) and signal reports from both parties. Grid reports from both parties are not required.
Saved "Decoded Messages" and "SWL Records" can be queried and exported from the web interface (accessible via a browser on the same local network).
Deleting saved "Decoded Messages" and "SWL Records" can be done through the "Clear Cache" option in the Settings page.
